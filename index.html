About Our School

A Legacy of Learning in the Heart of Baitadi

Durga Secondary School is one of the leading community schools in Baitadi District. Established in 2016 B.S., the school provides quality education from ECD (Early Childhood Development) to Grade 12. The school is affiliated with NEB and also offers a Technical Civil Engineering program from Grades 9–12 under the technical stream.

Our institution focuses on academic excellence, technical skills, discipline, leadership, innovation, and character development — shaping responsible citizens ready for higher education and professional careers.

  • ✔ Public Community School serving Simar & surrounding villages
  • ✔ NEB & CTEVT affiliated curriculum
  • ✔ Scholarship support for deserving students
  • ✔ Focus on discipline, leadership & innovation
